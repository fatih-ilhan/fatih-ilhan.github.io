I received my B.S. degree with high honors, and graduated with a CGPA of 3.81/4.00 from Bilkent University, Turkey in June 2019. I am currently an M.S. student at the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Bilkent University, under the supervision of Prof. Suleyman S. Kozat. I have also been working as a full-time machine learning engineer in DataBoss Security & Analytics for more than a year.

I have achieved the 191st rank nationwide in the National University Entrance Examinations among 2 million students. During my B.S. studies, I have been awarded the Comprehensive Fellowship by Bilkent University and JASSO Scholarship for my exchange studies in Nagoya University, Japan. During my M.S. studies, I have been awarded the TUBITAK Scholarship. I have co-authored 2 journal papers in highly respected Transactions, including IEEE Transactions, and 4 conference papers in refereed high impact conference proceedings. My research interests include machine learning, convex/non-convex optimization, anomaly detection, online learning, deep learning, big data, and their applications on forecasting, spatio-temporal prediction, computer vision and natural language processing.
